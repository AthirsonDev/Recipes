Cake

Um bolo é uma sobremesa doce, feito de farinha, açúcar, ovos e manteiga, com sabores adicionados como baunilha ou chocolate.

The Classic Vanilla Cake is a traditional favorite that never fails to satisfy. The recipe begins with the basics: butter, sugar, eggs, and flour. The butter is creamed with the sugar until it’s light and fluffy. Then, the eggs are added one at a time to ensure they’re well incorporated. The flour is mixed in gradually, alternating with milk to keep the batter smooth and creamy. A touch of vanilla extract adds a hint of delicate flavor that enhances the rich taste of the cake.

Once the batter is prepared, it’s poured into a greased and floured cake pan. The cake is then baked in a preheated oven at 350 degrees Fahrenheit (approximately 175 degrees Celsius) for about 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. After cooling, the cake can be frosted with your choice of icing, such as classic buttercream or cream cheese frosting, for a delicious and beautiful dessert. Enjoy!
